# 86 Chain Breaker

An 86 is a **state**, not a vibe. Define **who can 86**, **how it reaches every channel**, and **chain rules** so one outage does not silently kill three dishes.

**Leading words:** one source of 86 truth · 86 is a state · chain map · no silent 86

## Workflow

1. **One source of 86 truth** — default the POS 86 board. All other channels mirror it. **No silent 86** — grill-only knowledge does not count.
2. **Chain map** — for critical ingredients: which items die, which allow partial 86 (modifier), which get auto-sub from a chef-approved list.
3. **Role rights** — who may soft 86 (low stock warn), hard 86, un-86. Un-86 only with counted product, not hope.
4. **Channel blast script** — item · hard/soft · sub · ETA · who posted. Must hit expo, FOH floor lead, POS, online menus (checklist).
5. **Post-mortem lag watch** — wrong-order voids, guest "you sold me an 86," minutes from BOH 86 → POS 86. Agree a lag target for service.

## Controls

- Food safety > heroics — never sell past hold time.
- Online lag: pause item early if you cannot auto-sync rather than take orders you will void.
- Subs need allergen clean path; never free-sub into allergy risk.
- Public 86 wall language stays respectful — no guest names.

## Deliverables

1. Source-of-truth decision
2. Ingredient → dish chain map (top fragile items)
3. Soft vs hard 86 rights
4. Channel blast checklist + script
5. Lag scoreboard (BOH → POS → online)
